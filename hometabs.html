Corp2Corp.jobs is a comprehensive job search website with sophisticated and yet easy-to-use features such as e-recruiting like resume posting, position / job order posting, extensive search engine, internal / external recruiting management system, and account administration.

" This site offers paid and free services designed to bring together employers and career seekers."

How it Works

" The career site offer two levels of revenue generation. Paid postings and advertising."

" Employers can choose between account plans to post careers, that applicants can apply for."

" Employers receive instant notification of applications that are printable from the employers browser, plus employers can search resumes to find applicants for all their employee needs."

" For career seekers, membership is free. One the site career seekers can create a free resume that can be submitted for career postings by employers, plus searchable by employers."

" The career site also includes a rotating banner management system that is easily moderated from the admin area."
